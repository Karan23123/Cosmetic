Mi Commitments

MI VEGAN & CRUELTY-FREE

P.E.T.A:

MiDARE is certified by the People for Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) for its Beauty Without Bunny Program. PETA has classified MiDARE as a cruelty-free and vegan brand.

Leaping Bunny:

MiDARE Cosmetics is Leaping Bunny certified by the Coalition for Consumer Information on Cosmetics in both U.S. and Canada. This certifies that our products are 100% cruelty-free.

MI QUALITY

ISO 9001 - 2015:

MiDARE Cosmetics is committed to producing the highest quality products possible by following the International Quality Management System Standards.
MiDARE Cosmetics ingredients and material suppliers must be ISO 9001 certified to become MiDARE's business partners.

Good Manufacturing Practices:

All MiDARE's manufacturers are Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) certified and follow the GPM International Standards when producing MiDARE's products. Indeed, they all pledged to supply ingredients that are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, safe, and guarantee to have zero cross- contamination practices.

MI SAFETY

We pride ourselves on transparency and safe practices.

CLEAN FORMULATION - NO ARTIFICIAL FRAGRANCE - SENSITIVE-SKIN TYPES WON'T HAVE TO WORRY!

MiDARE's products never contain artificial fragrances. Our products are carefully created to be fragrance-free and guaranteed safe to use on even the most sensitive skin types. We mindfully select ingredients to be non- toxic to deliver on our promises of safe and efficient skincare products.

THE FOLLOWING INGREDIENTS ARE BANNED FROM MIDARE FORMULATIONS:

Artificial Colorants, Artificial Fragrances, Parabens, Ethanol, Ethyl Alcohol, Denatured Alcohol, Methanol, N-Butyl Alcohol, Isopropyl Alcohol, SD Alcohol, Lanolin, Propylene Glycol, Phthalates, Mineral Oil, Petrolatum, Formaldehydes, Oxybenzone, Coal Tar, Hydroquinone, Triclosan, Triclocarbon, Talc, Sulfates, and non-vegan ingredients.

VOLUNTARY COSMETICS REGISTRATION PROGRAM:

Upon launching MiDARE's on the U.S. market, all ingredients will be reported to the FDA to maintain transparency and safety. MiDARE Cosmetics pledge to participate in the Voluntary Cosmetics Registration Program to offer an extra layer of transparency.
