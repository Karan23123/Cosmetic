MI JOURNEY TO YOU!

MI INNOCENT DEBUT

Mes Débuts Innocents

The founder Of MiDARE Cosmetics, Sara Yahia, spent her early years in France where she was exposed to the personal care and beauty trends of the time. It is well known that the French believe in the notion of beauty being to please oneself above everything else. Sara was influenced by these beliefs while growing up, and they became a part Of her DNA.

MI CULTURAL REVELATION

Throughout Sara's adulthood, she was exposed to multicultural diversity and innovative thinking while living in New York. At that time, Sara struggled with acne and extremely dry skin. She knew there had to be a solution out there. This is when she first started her research and discovered life-changing K-beauty products. Sara knew this was something she wanted to pursue.

Later in life, Sara found herself working in Qatar as a Senior Director of Business Development and Human Operations in malls. Through this professional position, Sara was able to work closely with international beauty brands by helping them strategically penetrate and grow their market base. Sara dedicated her time understanding the different market strategies and business positions these large companies were adopting.

However, she could not shake the feeling that the market was missing something...

MI DARE NOW

Upon returning to the United States, Sara's extensive research and industry expertise led her down a path she had only dared to dream of. Indeed, she decided it was time to turn her dreams into a reality. During this chapter of her life, she built upon her extensive knowledge and skills to create and become CEO of MiDARE Cosmetics.

After this long journey, MiDARE Cosmetics was officially established in APRIL 2021
